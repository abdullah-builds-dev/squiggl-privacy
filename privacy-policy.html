Last updated: June 20, 2026

We collect the following personal information when you register or use the App:

We also collect usage data through Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This may include device type, app version, and in-app actions.

We use the information we collect to:

3. Payments

Squiggl offers in-app subscriptions processed through Google Play In-App Payments. We do not directly collect or store your payment details. All payment information is handled securely by Google Play. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers (such as Firebase) solely to operate and improve the App.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Squiggl is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
